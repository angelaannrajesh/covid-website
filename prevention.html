How To Protect Yourself

Protection from Covid is essential. Save lives.

You should do

  • stay at home

  • wear mask

  • use sanitizer

  • disinfect your home

  • wash your hands

  • frequent self-isolation

You should avoid

  • avoid infected people

  • avoid animals

  • avoid handshaking

  • avoid infected surfaces

  • dont touch your face

  • avoid droplets
